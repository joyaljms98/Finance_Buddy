# Understanding Mutual Funds

A comprehensive guide to starting your mutual fund investment journey. Learn the basics of NAV, loads, and compounding returns.

Mutual funds pool money from the investing public and use that money to buy other securities, usually stocks and bonds. The value of the mutual fund company depends on the performance of the securities it decides to buy. 

## Why invest in Mutual Funds?
1. **Professional Management**: The funds are managed by experts.
2. **Diversification**: It reduces the risk of loss.
3. **Liquidity**: You can easily buy and sell your shares.
